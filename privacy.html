Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Drive Dashboard is a browser-based tool that connects to your Google Drive to display and manage file sharing permissions.

What data we access

When you sign in, Drive Dashboard requests access to your Google Drive in order to:

  • List your files and folders
  • Read sharing permissions on your files
  • Add, update, or remove sharing permissions on your behalf when you take an action in the app

How your data is used

All data is processed entirely in your browser. Drive Dashboard does not have a backend server. Your files, permissions, and Google credentials are never transmitted to or stored on any third-party server.

Data storage

Your Google access token is temporarily stored in your browser's session storage and is cleared when you close the tab or sign out. No data is stored in cookies or on any external server.

Third-party services

Drive Dashboard uses the following Google services:

  • Google Identity Services — for authentication
  • Google Drive API v3 — for reading and managing your files

Your use of these services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Revoking access

You can revoke Drive Dashboard's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions.
